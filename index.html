General entertainment authority

The project involves the development of a robust database system for the General Entertainment Authority (GEA) to effectively manage and organize entertainment-related data. Faced with challenges of disorganized and dispersed information, the GEA aims to create a centralized database that enhances data integrity, security, and accessibility. By streamlining these interactions, the project seeks to improve insights and decision-making in the entertainment sector, ultimately elevating the overall entertainment experience.
